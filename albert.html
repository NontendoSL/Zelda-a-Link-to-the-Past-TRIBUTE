Contribution

Q&A

As a Q&A lead, my task has consisted in doing several testing sessions in order to improve the quality of the game itself. I created an issue template so any member could rapidly report any issue they found. Also, Sergio Sáez and Yabel (a former member of the team) have been doing Q&A sessions aswell in order to support my task. I cannot thank them enough.

Other tasks

There was so much work in many other aspects of the game, so I have had to do tasks that were not strictly my direct responsibility. I haven't coded much, but I have collaborated in small things such as combat in Zelda and Pokemon Worlds. EX: Soldier and Golem behaviour, Link state machine (hurt), and I also have coded some sound effects. I have collaborated a lot with Sergio Álvarez in art tasks. I have created some maps using Tiled, I have gathered lots of game sounds and themes, as well as sprites.
